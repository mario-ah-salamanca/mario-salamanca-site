# Project Brief

## Website Mission

Mario Salamanca’s website is a long-term personal brand platform for career growth, professional credibility, consulting/freelance leads, and founder positioning.

## Primary Audiences

- Recruiters
- Hiring managers
- Startup founders
- Small business owners
- Creators
- Technical collaborators
- Future clients

## Brand Attributes

- Technical
- Reliable
- Ambitious
- Creative
- Human
- Premium
- Systems-minded

## Site Goals

1. Explain who Mario is.
2. Show what Mario can build.
3. Prove technical competence.
4. Generate professional opportunities.
5. Capture leads.
6. Build long-term reputation.

## Current Website Stage

Production V1 landing page.

## Target Website Stage

Complete personal brand platform with homepage, case studies, services, resources, writing, resume, and contact funnel.
